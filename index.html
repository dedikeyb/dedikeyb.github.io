About

Started playing music independently since 1992 and seriously pursued audio production since 2005. Expanded into visual production and creative technology while continuously exploring innovation in music, sound, and storytelling. As a creative producer from Bengkulu, every work becomes an effort to prove that creativity from the regions can stand proudly on national and international stages.

Creative Journey

1992
Started learning and playing music independently.

2005
Began professional audio production and studio activities.

2006
Worked on regional music production projects with TVRI and explored visual production.

2007 – 2008
Studied Mixing & Mastering at Dolphindaw Jakarta.

2009 – Present
Produced mars, theme songs, regional music productions, TVC music, company profiles, and brand campaigns.

2020 – Present
Expanded into VST instrument development, AI, audio technology, and large-scale national events including MPL Indonesia, PUBG Mobile, and Free Fire.
